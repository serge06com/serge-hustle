How to Make Money/Week Selling Digital Products in South Africa

By Serge Hustle • July 2025

What Are Digital Products?

Digital products are items that are sold online without a physical form. These include eBooks, courses, templates, printables, stock photos, music beats, and more.

Why Sell Digital Products?

  • Low startup cost (no inventory or shipping)
  • Can be sold over and over again
  • Passive income potential
  • Fully online – no office required

Popular Digital Products You Can Sell in SA

  1. eBooks: Write about what you know – business, motivation, cooking, study guides.
  2. Templates: CV templates, business plans, invoice templates.
  3. Online Courses: Teach skills like coding, design, makeup, photography, etc.
  4. Presets & Filters: Sell Lightroom presets, Instagram filters.
  5. Printables: Journals, planners, vision boards.

Where to Sell Your Digital Products

  • Gumroad.com: Easy for South Africans and accepts PayPal.
  • PayHip.com: Simple store setup with free plans.
  • WhatsApp + EFT: Sell directly in groups and status.
  • Shopify: More advanced, but powerful store builder.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Choose a product type (e.g., eBook or course)
  2. Create your product (use Google Docs, Canva, etc.)
  3. Open a Gumroad or PayHip store
  4. Set your price (start with R50–R150)
  5. Promote on social media (TikTok, WhatsApp, Facebook)

Tips to Make Weekly Sales

  • Share reviews or testimonials
  • Create TikTok videos explaining your product
  • Bundle your products (e.g. 3 templates for R100)
  • Offer discounts to new buyers

Final Thoughts

With just your smartphone and creativity, you can start earning weekly income by selling digital products. Build your brand, stay consistent, and reinvest your earnings to grow even more!
