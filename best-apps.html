Best Apps That Pay South Africans in 2025

Looking to earn money using your phone? Here are the best apps that pay South Africans in 2025:

1. SnapnSave

Get cashback on groceries by snapping your till slips. Withdraw your earnings to your bank account.

2. SurveySavvy

Complete surveys and get paid via PayPal. Great for earning extra cash in your free time.

3. Toluna

Share your opinions and earn rewards or cash. Redeem points for vouchers or PayPal payments.

4. Fiverr

Offer freelance services like writing, design, or video editing and get paid by clients worldwide.

5. Yoco Go

Accept card payments for your business and manage your earnings easily with the Yoco app.

6. Field Agent

Complete small tasks like checking prices or taking photos in stores and get paid instantly.

7. Upwork

Find freelance jobs in writing, tech, design, and more. Get paid in dollars and withdraw to your local account.
